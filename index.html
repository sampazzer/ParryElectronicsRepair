I’m Sam, here’s a bit about me.

I completed my apprenticeship in component-level electronics repair for military aircraft. Most of my career has been spent as an automation and controls technician in pharmaceutical manufacturing. I have a long-standing passion for repairing things as a hobby, and I now want to offer that experience to others.

About the Service

I offer a straightforward repair service with a focus on quality workmanship and clear communication. I'll keep you informed throughout the process so theres no shock costs. Whether you need a one-off repair or help diagnosing a recurring issue, I am happy to take a look and explain the options clearly.
